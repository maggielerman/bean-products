Dual-Filtration System

01

Molekule comes with two filters: A Pre-Filter and a Nano-Filter. The Pre-Filter captures larger particles like dust and pet hair and slows down VOCs on their way to the Nano-Filter. The Nano-Filter is coated with our proprietary catalyst that breaks down VOCs, bacteria, viruses, and other pollutants at a molecular level when activated by light. .

02

Molekule’s $129 a year filter subscription ensures that replacement filters are conveniently delivered to doorstep when they are needed. On average, the Nano-Filter should be replaced once every six months and the Pre-Filter every three months. More information can be found here.
